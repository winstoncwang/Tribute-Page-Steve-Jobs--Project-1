Timeline

  • 1955 - Steven Paul Jobs is born in San Francisco to Joanne Carole Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali. The couple give up their son to adoption. Paul and Clara Jobs become Jobs' non-biological parents.

  • 1961 - Jobs family moves to Mountain View, California, later known as Silicon Valley.

  • 1968 - Bill Hewlett looking gor spare parts to build a frequency counter. This gave Jobs the opportunity of an internship.

  • 1970 - Jobs meets future Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.

  • 1976 - Graduates from Homestead High School and later work as an engineer at Atari. Co-founds Apple Computer with Wozniak & Ronald Wayne. The Apple I sells fro $666.66.

  • 1977 - The Apple II, the first commercially available personal computer/sucessful personal computer.

  • 1980 - Apple goes public, putting Jobs' net worth of $200 million.

  • 1986 - CEO John Sculley take over, Jobs resigns as Apple chairman. Jobs starts NeXT Computer by selling his $70 million worth of Apple stock. For $10 million, buys Pixar Animation Studios.

  • 1997 - Returns from Apple, as an adviser and become CEO, later as interim chief in September.

  • 2001 - Introduces iTunes for Mac users. Apple ships the first version of Mac OS X, coded-named Cheetah. Apple introduces the iPod and sold more than 300 million as of October 2011.

  • 2004 - Apple opens the iTunes Music Store in the US. Jobs udergoes surgery to remove a cancerous tumor in his pancreas.

  • 2006 - Apple's market capitalisation surpasses Dell's. Disney buys Pixar for $7.4 billion. iTunes sells its billionth song.

  • 2007 - Drops "Computer" from Apple's name. Introduces the iPhone. Owning 19.2 percent of the smartphone market.

  • 2019 - Apple introduces App Store, unveils MobileMe, Apple's first cloud offering. Jobs takes a 6-month leave of absence for medical reason. Apple introduces the iPad.

  • 2010 - Jobs goes on medical leave of absence from CEO post nearly two years after the six-month break he took to undergo a liver transplant.

  • 2011 - At his last keynote at WWDC 2011. Steve Jobs resigns as CEO of Apple. Steve Jobs dies at home, surrounded by his family.
